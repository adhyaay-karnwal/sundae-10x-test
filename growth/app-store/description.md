Stop spending Sundays playing macro Tetris.

Sundae is the weekly-prep co-pilot for gym-goers, lifters, and anyone serious about hitting their numbers. Tell it your targets and it builds a 7-day plan, the grocery list, and a Sunday prep schedule that actually adds up.

*Plan once. Eat right all week.*

**What Sundae does**

- Builds a 7-day meal plan tuned to your kcal and protein targets
- Scales servings per recipe and totals macros by day
- Generates an auto grocery list grouped by aisle
- Subtracts what you already have via pantry tracking
- Walks you through Sunday prep in batch order: chop, roast, simmer, portion

**Why it works**

Sundae is built around one ritual: a single weekly planning session that pays off across seven days of eating. No bite-by-bite logging. No social feed. Just a tight loop from plan to grocery run to prep day to plate.

Designed for *recomp, cut, and lean-bulk* — serious tools, no clutter.
