# Publishing

These files are generated by 10x for Sundae. Review them before publishing or submitting to Apple.

## Files

- `privacy-policy.md`: Privacy Policy draft.
- `terms-of-service.md`: Terms of Service draft.
- `support.md`: Support page draft.
- `app-privacy-guidance.md`: App Store Connect App Privacy guidance.
- `review-notes.md`: Apple reviewer notes.
- `submission.json`: Full structured submission packet.

## Submission URLs

Custom URLs entered in 10x take precedence. Blank custom URLs fall back to hosted `apps.10x.app` pages after publishing.
